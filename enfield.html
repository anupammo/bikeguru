Royal Enfield

Royal Enfield is a renowned motorcycle manufacturer based in India. The company has a rich history that dates back to 1893 when it was established in Redditch, England. Initially, Royal Enfield produced motorcycles, bicycles, and stationary engines. Royal Enfield gained popularity for its rugged motorcycles and powerful engines. Over the years, it has become an iconic brand known for its classic and vintage-inspired designs. The distinctive thumping sound produced by its engines has also become synonymous with the Royal Enfield brand. In 1955, the production of Royal Enfield motorcycles shifted entirely to India.
