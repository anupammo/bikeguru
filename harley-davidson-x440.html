At the heart of the Harley Davidson X440 beats a powerhouse of an engine. The seamless power delivery allows the X440 to effortlessly conquer highways and conquer city streets alike, making it an ideal choice for riders who demand both performance and versatility.

True to the Harley Davidson tradition, the X440 showcases a blend of classic styling and modern aesthetics. The seamless integration of design elements, from the iconic headlamp to the gracefully contoured fuel tank, exudes a sense of timelessness that is unmistakably Harley Davidson.

Technology and Features:

The Harley Davidson X440 doesn't just rely on raw power; it embraces cutting-edge technology to enhance the riding experience. The digital instrument cluster offers a wealth of information at a glance, including speed, fuel levels, and navigation data. Smartphone connectivity allows riders to stay connected while on the road, ensuring both convenience and safety.

Long journeys become a pleasure rather than a challenge on the X440, thanks to its thoughtful ergonomics. The well-designed seat, handlebars, and footpegs ensure rider comfort even during extended rides. The balanced chassis and suspension contribute to a smooth and controlled ride, absorbing bumps and imperfections with finesse.
